ABOUT US

The information and communication technology club of Federal Science and technical College kafanchan was introduced by the school management, supervised by the club and society co-ordinator and managed by the club patrons. With these, the aim is to bring the interest of ICT to students (Gender Equality) in the college and to enhanced the creativity of students in the aspect of robotics, web development, graphics and stand alone application to solve our daily problems. In respect to the aim of the club, the students has participated in various ICT school conpetitions such as robotics games in different years and online programming classes, TECNO Vision organized for the female students to bring their interest into information and communication technology.
